Bakery

Handmade Artisan Breads
– Baked Fresh Every Morning –

A new addition to our café as of January 2021, we encourage you to follow the enticing aromas of freshly baked bread and wander downstairs to our bakery.

Currently Offering:

  • • Sourdough
  • • Flavoured Sourdough
  • • Italian Pagnotta
  • • Malt Bread (Wednesdays)
  • • Rye Bread (Tuesdays)
  • • White & Multigrain Bread
  • • Raisin Bread
  • • Garlic Cheese Bread
  • • Kerri Buns - white & multigrain
  • • Montreal-Style Bagels

Each of our recipes have been meticulously tested and are baked fresh every morning in our stone-deck oven.

We are also proud to say that our breads are preservative-free, so you can have peace of mind that you are nourishing your body with the very best!
